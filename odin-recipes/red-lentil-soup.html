Red Lentil 'Bride' Soup (Düğün Çorbasi)

A traditional Turkish soup. It's called Bride Soup (Düğün Çorbası) in Turkish language because, they make this soup on their wedding days.

Easy-to-cook and tasty.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 cup red lentils
  • ½ cup fine bulgur
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 8 cups vegetable stock
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon dried mint leaves
  • 4 slices lemon
  • ½ teaspoon chopped fresh mint
